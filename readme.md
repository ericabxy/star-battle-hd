# Star Battle HD

_Star Battle HD_ is a two-dimensional sprite-based space shooter played from a top-down view with 360&deg; of freedom.

This game is programmed in Lua for personal computers using the LÖVE framework. Eschewing the limitations required to work with the Lutro core for LibRetro, _Star Battle HD_ runs a visual simulation that can take full advantage of LÖVE capabilities including high-resolution, rotating, and scaling textures.

Gameplay
--------

The player takes control of a dual-nacelle torchship alongside a fleet of six sister ships. Activity around the shipyard is at a minimum at this time of the galactic turning, so you are free to take your command on practice maneuvers and shakedowns. The central space station's inertial pressors are more than enough to avoid colliding with debris and asteroids that intersect the zone, so such objects are available for target practice.

* Left/Right Keys: Rotational thrusters.
* Up Key: Forward thrusters.
* 'S' Key: Fire graser projector.
